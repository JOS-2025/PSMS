# PSMS Pro - User Guide

Welcome to **PSMS Pro**, the Petrol Station Management System. This guide will help you navigate and use the application effectively.

## 1. Getting Started
### Login
- Access the application URL.
- Log in using your registered email and password.
- If you don't have an account, contact your administrator.

## 2. Dashboard
The Dashboard provides a high-level overview of your operations:
- **Total Sales**: Real-time revenue tracking.
- **Inventory Status**: Quick view of fuel levels in tanks.
- **Active Shifts**: Monitor which staff members are currently on duty.
- **Recent Activity**: A feed of the latest transactions and events.

## 3. Shift Management
Shifts are the core of the system. Most operations (sales, expenses) require an active shift.
- **Start Shift**: Go to the **Shifts** tab. Select your station, enter the opening meter reading, and click "Start Shift".
- **End Shift**: At the end of your duty, enter the closing meter reading and the total cash collected. The system will automatically calculate discrepancies.
- **View Details**: Click the "Eye" icon in the Shift History to see every transaction recorded during that specific shift.

## 4. Shift Operations (Sales)
This is where you record daily fuel and lubricant sales.
- **Fuel Sales**: Select the pump, enter the liters sold, and choose a payment method (Cash, M-Pesa, or Credit).
- **Lubricant Sales**: Record sales of engine oils and other products.
- **Credit Sales**: If a customer is buying on credit, you must select the registered customer from the list.

## 5. Inventory & Tanks
- **Tank Levels**: Monitor real-time fuel levels in the **Inventory** tab.
- **Record Dip Reading**: Perform physical dipstick measurements and record them to compare against system readings.
- **Deliveries**: View history of fuel deliveries to the station.

## 6. Lubricants Management
- **Inventory**: Track stock levels for all lubricant products across stations.
- **Restock**: Record new deliveries of lubricants.
- **Catalog**: View the standard prices and categories of available lubricants.

## 7. Credit & Debtors
- **New Customer**: Register corporate clients or regular credit customers.
- **Transactions**: Record payments made by customers or manual credit purchases.
- **Balances**: Monitor current balances against credit limits.

## 8. Expenses
- **Record Expense**: Log operational costs like electricity, water, or maintenance.
- **Shift Association**: Expenses recorded during an active shift are automatically deducted from the expected cash for that shift.

## 9. Reports
- **Daily Reports**: Detailed breakdown of sales, volumes, and variances for any specific day.
- **Performance Tracking**: Analyze trends across different stations and products.

## 10. Management & Staff
*(Admin Only)*
- **Stations**: Add or edit petrol station locations.
- **Pumps & Tanks**: Configure the hardware layout of each station.
- **Staff**: Manage user accounts and permissions.
